Our Story

A warm Greek table inside Toronto's iconic market.

Yianni's Kitchen is a family-owned Greek restaurant located in Toronto's iconic St. Lawrence Market. For more than two decades we have been serving authentic Greek dishes made with fresh ingredients and traditional family recipes.

Whether you're joining us for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or catering for a special event, our goal is to bring the warmth of Greek hospitality to every guest.
